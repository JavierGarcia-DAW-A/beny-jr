BIOGRAPHY

Ei que pasa soy Beny JR. Born and raised in Barcelona, with roots that reach from Spain to Morocco. Music has always been my way to tell the truth, to turn real life into rhythm and words.



I started making songs with my people, just for fun, recording in small studios and writing what I lived every day. No filters, no lies — just real stories from my neighborhood, from my world.



Over time, that energy grew into something bigger. Tracks like “Combo La L”, “Andando”, and “Flow Italiano” started to connect with people everywhere — because they felt the same hunger, the same pain, the same drive to make it out.



For me, music isn’t about fame. It’s about loyalty, family, and staying true to who you are. Every line I write is part of my journey — from the block to the stage, from the silence to the sound.

MY HOOD, MY REALS

This is my last track with my brother Morad, here we saw you our lifestyle and all the people who always has been there. The values that the street taught us. The importance of respect but without letting them step on you, the humility of knowing where you come from and not forgetting the people who fed you.
